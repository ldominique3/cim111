Ways to Get Involved

Volunteer

Volunteers are always needed at cancer centers, hospices, and hospitals. Volunteers can be used to complete tasks that are needed to keep organizations going. All you have to do is contact a local breast cancer organization and there will find a job for you to do.

Donate

There are so many non-profit organizations in which people can donate their money to fight against breast cancer. You can even get involved in breast cancer races as a way to truly give of yourself. People donate anywhere from $5, to a full head of hair, in support of the cure.

Use What You've Got

One of the best ways to get involved is to use your talents to brighten up a patientâ€™s day. Whether it is knitting warm blankets or singing for a chemo patient, there is an array of talents that can be used to make a difference.

Fundraise

Fundraising can take place in many forms. Thousands od people across the nation raise money to find the cure by getting involved in carwashes, bake sales, and more. All money counts.

Share Stories

Sharing the stories of breast cancer survivors and those who have died from the disease helps to educate people about breast cancer, along with showing people each special individual journey.
