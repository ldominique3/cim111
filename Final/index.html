Welcome to Pink Ribbon Stories

October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month, a time where people all over the nation honor the millions of women and men who have been diagnosed with the disease, and raise money to end it. Today there are more than “3.1 million women with a history of breast cancer in the U.S and an estimated 1 in 8 women will develop a form of breast cancer over the course of their life,” making awareness imperative (breastcancer.org). This wbesite is used to spread the stories of breast cancer survivors, as well as thos who lost their lives to cancer. It is one thing to know what breast cancer is, but it is another to know the patient's stories.
