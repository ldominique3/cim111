How To Make a Rat Chop

What is a Rat Chop? (Mouse over for the answer)

Ingredients?

  • Yellow Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Grilled Chicken
  • Curry Mayo

Steps?

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Place 2 cups of yellow rice so that it fills 1/4 of your chosen bowl
  3. Add 1 cup of grilled chicken to the third corner of your bowl
  4. Add 1/2 a cup of tomatoes to one corner of your bowl
  5. Add 1/2 a cup of lettuce to another corner of yout bowl (the idea is to make the bowl look cute
  6. Finally add two containers full of curry mayo to your dish
  7. Now it's up to you, you can mix it, add more ingrediants, but the most important step is to enjoy!!!
